Mission Statement

Our mission is to raise awareness of certain issues in underdeveloped nations. We have selected these countries due to the gravity of these issues and the impact they have on the country regarding all aspects of life. Through this website we hope to inform the public and show them ways they can help. We realize that it is sometimes difficult to learn about something through text or even photos; we hope to enhance the learning experience using virtual reality. With the use of VR, users can more easily immerse themselves into the various situations and gain more insight into these issues.
